2. AI Features (Key Differences)

Classic — Galaxy Watch 3

  • Manual or interval-based readings
  • No predictive analysis
  • No AI sleep or stress coaching

AI-powered — Galaxy Watch 8

  • Bedtime Guidance - AI predicts your ideal sleep/wake timing based on circadian patterns and sleep history.
  • Sleep Coaching - Multi-week coaching plan that analyzes your sleep patterns and provides personalized improvement tips.
  • Vascular Load - AI analysis of vascular stiffness and pulse wave patterns during sleep, providing cardiovascular load insights.
  • ECG and Rhythm Notifications - On-wrist ECG with automated irregular rhythm alerts powered by AI rhythm classification.
  • Energy Score - Daily readiness level calculated from sleep, activity, stress, and recovery metrics using machine-learning models.
  • Running Coach (AI-enhanced) - Real-time adaptive running feedback, personalized plans, and recommended intensities.
  • Medication Tracking - Smart reminders based on habit prediction and adherence patterns.
  • Antioxidant Index - AI interprets carotenoid measurements from skin to estimate antioxidant levels and lifestyle quality.
